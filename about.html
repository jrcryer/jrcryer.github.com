About

Intro

Hello there! My name is James Cryer and welcome to my personal website and blog - the home of my thoughts and ideas on the latest trends in software engineering for the web and mobile.

I am based in Cardiff, UK - where I have lived for over the past 10 years.

History

Since graduating from Cardiff University with a 1st class degree in Computer science, I have worked in the web industry.

Over the last 6 years, I have honed my skills across several platforms and languages to reach the level of Senior Software Engineer. My long term goal is to become a CTO / Director for a software house.

I pride myself on being socialable developer and prefer a face to face conversation than one conducted over Skype/Google Talk.

I follow the ethos of learning something new everyday which I think it is essential for the success into today's web industry.

Four years of my professional career was spent at Box UK, an excellant software company based in Cardiff. Whilst at Box UK, I lead on several medium to large projects for financial, leisure and education sectors .

With the salient growth in the mobile market, I have spent the past 2 years further my skillset to include native mobile application. During 2012, I collaborated on a project to deliver an application for both iOS and Android marketplace for a major UK retailer.

Currently, I am a Senior Web Developer at BBC Future Media for Travel News product. This is a new opportunity that I started in September 2013.

Aside from the above, I have also worked passionately with small / medium businesses over the past 18 months to development and deliver software solutions to enable them to meet the needs of their clients.

To see more details of my education and employment history, please visit my LinkedIn profile.
